About

I am a PhD scholar at the Transportation Research and Injury Prevention Centre (TRIP‑C), IIT Delhi. My research focuses on the emergence and integration of Micromobility (PBS, e‑bikes, e‑scooters) in India—combining large‑scale GPS datasets, GIS, and econometric models to inform safer, more equitable infrastructure and policy.

Related: policy portal → Micromobility_Policy

Research Interests

PBS station siting & density Mode choice & demand modelling Crash & safety analytics Spatial lag / Durbin Policy benchmarking

  • Trip generation & station influence

    NB/ZINB models with land‑use, income proxies (night‑lights), and POIs; equity & accessibility overlays.

  • Large‑scale GPS parsing

    Trajectory cleaning, stop/move segmentation, OD inference, and rebalancing analytics for delivery e‑scooters.

  • Policy & implementation mapping

    Country‑wise micromobility regulations compared with Indian context; gaps & roadmaps.
